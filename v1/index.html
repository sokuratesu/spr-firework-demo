Taxonomy

Six-Dimension Hashtag Structure

Every video is tagged with hashtags from six dimensions. This enables flexible filtering across all pages.

Dimension Name Example Hashtags Purpose
D1 Product Category pe he is Top-level category filtering
D2 Sub Category headphone tv soundbar Video Hub category tabs
D3 Series bravia-3 linkbuds-clip Personalization by registered product series
D4 SKU wfc900b xr-a95l Personalization by specific model
D5 Video Format short video Shorts tab vs Videos tab
D6 Content Type setup tutorial feature Content purpose filtering

Post-Registration Video (Hero Unit)

After a customer registers a BRAVIA 3 TV, we show them a setup video using Hero Unit. The embed filters by hashtags matching their registered product.

<fw-storyblock channel="sony_electronics_us" hashtag="(and he tv bravia-3 setup)" max_videos="1" ></fw-storyblock>
